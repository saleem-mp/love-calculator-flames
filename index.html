What is the FLAMES Love Calculator?

The FLAMES love calculator is a fun relationship compatibility test based on the popular FLAMES game that has been enjoyed by generations. FLAMES stands for Friends, Lovers, Affectionate, Marriage, Enemies, and Siblings - the six possible relationship outcomes between two people.

How Does the FLAMES Calculator Work?

The FLAMES algorithm works by analyzing the letters in both names to determine your compatibility:

  • Step 1: Remove all common letters from both names
  • Step 2: Count the remaining letters
  • Step 3: Use this count to eliminate letters from the word "FLAMES" in sequence
  • Step 4: The remaining letter reveals your relationship outcome

About the FLAMES Game

FLAMES is a popular relationship prediction game that originated as a paper-and-pen game. It became especially popular among school children and teenagers as a fun way to explore potential relationships. While it's not scientifically validated, it remains an entertaining way to think about relationships and compatibility.

Understanding Your FLAMES Result

Each letter in FLAMES represents a different type of relationship:

  • F - Friends: A strong, trusting friendship
  • L - Lovers: Romantic connection with deep affection
  • A - Affectionate: Caring and warm relationship
  • M - Marriage: Potential for long-term commitment
  • E - Enemies: Possible conflicts or misunderstandings
  • S - Siblings: Brotherly/sisterly bond

FAQs

Q: Is the FLAMES calculator accurate?
A: FLAMES is meant for entertainment purposes only. While it can be fun to see your result, it shouldn't be taken as a serious relationship predictor.

Q: Can I use nicknames?
A: Yes! You can use full names, nicknames, or any combination you prefer. The calculator works with whatever names you choose to enter.

Q: Why do I get different results with the same names?
A: Make sure you're entering the names exactly the same way each time, including spaces and special characters. Small differences can change the calculation.
